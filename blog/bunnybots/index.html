BunnyBots!

Caleb Eby September 8, 2016

Bunnybots has begun! Find out about the 2016 BunnyBots competition, "Round-a-BOUT"

The Competition

BunnyBots is an annual competition for FRC teams hosted by the Flaming Chickens, at Catlin Gabel. The 12-week build season starts in September, and lasts until December. Like FRC, the game changes every year.

2016 Game

The 2016 game, Round-a-BOUT, was announced September 1st. In it, six robots, three per alliance, drive around a figure-eight track while shooting Nerf darts at each other, and loading stuffed bunnies into recycle bins.

What we're up to

We are very busy coming up with ideas for this year's BunnyBot. We'll share more in a few weeks!
