What is our challenge for 2017?

Early this morning we drove to University of Portland to find out what our game was for 2017.

Predictions

Before kickoff, we knew that there was a steampunk theme because of the teaser video After that, Alan and I scoured the internet for the most reasonable theories of the 2017 game, STEAMWORKS. The best we could find was from Cothron Theiss on CD.

The Game

In summary, we have to score 10” gears on hooks and 5” balls in boilers. We are super excited for this new game and season. We plan on releasing weekly updates on how we are doing.
